# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The primary users are university research groups, hospital research teams, biotech or pharma R&D staff, and enterprise procurement or business contacts. They arrive with a practical need: find a technical service, submit a reagent or consumable procurement request, or contact the business team for formal follow-up.

## Product Purpose

Subo Biotech's frontend presents Guangzhou Subo Biotech as a scientific service and procurement coordination partner. The site should make three actions equally clear: enter the quote center, submit procurement needs, and contact business support. Success means a qualified visitor can understand the scope, choose the right path, and submit a request without needing a login or a long sales conversation first.

## Brand Personality

Precise, high-trust, high-impact. The interface should feel scientific and capable, with more technological force than the current version, while staying credible for medical, academic, and procurement audiences.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS hero sections, soft pastel biotech decoration, ornamental glass cards, three identical feature cards, fake laboratory dashboards, poetic agency-style labels, and copy that sounds like abstract marketing. The site should not feel like a template or a personal portfolio.

## Design Principles

1. Three paths, equal weight: quote center, procurement request, and business contact should all be visible as first-class actions.
2. Scientific confidence before persuasion: prioritize service scope, process clarity, response expectations, and formal business handoff.
3. Technology with discipline: use strong contrast, precise geometry, and purposeful motion without making the site feel like a game or speculative AI product.
4. Fast judgment: each major surface should help visitors decide what to do next in one screen.
5. Preserve business reality: keep existing routes, Chinese business copy, no-login inquiry flow, and company identity recognizable unless an explicit redesign decision changes them.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for all text and controls. The site should remain usable for procurement and research users under normal office conditions, on desktop and mobile. Motion should respect reduced-motion preferences, and form-like surfaces should keep labels and action text clear in Chinese.
